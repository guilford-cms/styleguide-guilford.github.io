MY GUILFORD NOTES

Save the things that are important to you, and come back here to view them anytime.

To get started, look for the Guilford Notes icon next to majors, faculty names, page titles and in other places throughout the site. Click it to save the item to your notes.

The first time you do this, you will be asked to create your personal login.

You can also save snippets of text. Simply highlight what you want to keep and click on the Notes icon that appears. This is great for deadlines and application instructions.

Return to your notes anytime by clicking the Guilford Notes icon in the top right corner of your device.

Happy saving!
