Tips

A tip is showing to the user as an icon that is added to certain components throughout the page template. Once clicked/touched, a pop-up is exposed that gives further information about the subject or a sample experience by one of Guilford’s faculty, staff or students. Tips are displayed at the end of a components title as an optional inline item. The following components allow for the addition of a tip: Feature Teaser, Smart Teaser & Intro.
