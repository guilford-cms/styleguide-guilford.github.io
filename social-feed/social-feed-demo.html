Social Feed

The social feed follows the basic functionality of the news feed. The event feed displays the latest 10 social posts based on the accounts entered in drupal. Cards can be one of 2 sizes (2x2, or 1x1) depending on the position it occupies in the grid and the device it is being viewed on. The card sizes are in a static position in the grid, but posts can change position naturally as the feed updates. The newest post will begin in position #1.
